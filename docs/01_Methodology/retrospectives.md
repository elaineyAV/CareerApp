# Retrospectives

## Purpose

Regular team retrospectives to reflect on our process and continuously improve. These are internal team meetings, separate from stakeholder reviews.

**Schedule**: Every 2 weeks throughout the project

**Focus**:
- What went well
- What didn't go well  
- What we learned
- Actions to improve

## Retrospectives vs. Stakeholder Reviews

**Team Retrospectives** (this document):
- Internal team meetings
- Focus on process improvement
- Bi-weekly schedule: Nov 12, Nov 26, Dec 10, Dec 24 (if needed), Final
- Purpose: Improve how we work together

**Stakeholder Reviews** (with instructor):
- External progress reviews
- Focus on product progress and technical approach
- Schedule: Late November (mid-project), Late December (final)
- Purpose: Get feedback and demonstrate progress
